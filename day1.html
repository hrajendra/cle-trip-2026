One terminal. Pick up the rental car — aim for wheels-down by 4pm ET to allow buffer before dinner. It's 15–20 min from plane to car, then 20 min west on I-90 to the Airbnb.

📌 Drop bags at the Airbnb first, freshen up, then head to Ohio City for dinner.

6:30 PM
Dinner
Soho Chicken + Whiskey
4211 Lorain Ave, Ohio City
Reservation required Open until 9pm Fri $$
Southern fried chicken, elevated. High-ceilinged, buzzy room in Ohio City. Strong whiskey list — the cocktails are just as good as the food. One of the best spots in Cleveland.
Must Order
  • Fried green tomato with elote — the standout of the menu
  • Fried chicken (bone-in) with Mac & cheese and collard greens
  • Pimento cheese and fancy deviled eggs to start
  • Whatever whiskey cocktail the bartender recommends

⚠️ Reservation essential — call (216) 298-9090 or book at sohocleveland.com. Confirm a day before.

9:00 PM
Scotch Bar
The Whiskey Bar
2767 Euclid Heights Blvd, Cleveland Heights
Open til 2am Fri Closed Sundays 15 min from Soho
A wall of whisky greets you at the door. The bartenders know their stuff — they'll walk you through a guided scotch flight with palate cleansers between pours. Two levels, relaxed atmosphere, no rush. A hidden gem in Cleveland Heights.
How to Approach It
  • Tell the bartender your usual scotch — peaty, sherried, or lighter — and let them build a flight
  • Ask for the palate cleanser between pours — they do this well
  • Wednesday nights have customised flight specials (not tonight, but worth noting for future)

🚗 Plan a rideshare home from here. Don't drive after a scotch flight — Uber/Lyft run well in Cleveland Heights.

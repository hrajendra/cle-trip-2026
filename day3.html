The standout trail of the weekend. Sharon Conglomerate rock formations — ancient, dramatic, unlike anything in the Brandywine area. The outer loop counterclockwise is the right call: overlook first, then the cave section.

🧊 Do Icebox Cave. It's a short detour off the main loop — a narrow passage through rock that stays year-round cold. Adds 20 min and is the best part of the hike.

Trail Notes
  • Large paved parking lot — arrive by 8am to get a good spot
  • Do the outer loop counterclockwise (3:00 on the map, then left) for the best sequence
  • Overlook is an easy 10-min walk from the lot — don't skip it
  • Bring water — no fountains on trail
  • Roots and rocks on the cave section — trail shoes earn their keep here
🚗 Drive to Tremont ~30 min. The South Side opens at 10am Sundays — timing works perfectly.

The South Side

A beloved Tremont neighbourhood spot with big windows, great natural light, and a Sunday brunch menu that over-delivers. The smash burgers and nachos are the stars. If the patio's open, take it.
Must Order
  • Smash burger — some of the best in Cleveland
  • Nachos — legitimately great, not an afterthought
  • Mini donut flight — described by regulars as "a spiritual experience"
  • Biscuits and gravy — if you're still going

✈️ Arrival — Friday May 8
At CLE Airport

Land at Cleveland Hopkins (CLE). Pick up the rental car — it's all one terminal, easy to navigate. Takes 15–20 min from wheels-down to car.

Drive to the Airbnb to drop bags — Westlake is ~20 min west of the airport on I-90.

Getting to Soho Chicken + Whiskey

4211 Lorain Ave, Ohio City — about 20 min east from Westlake. Park on Lorain Ave or nearby side streets (usually free evenings).

📌 Reservation is essential. Confirm the booking a day before as a reminder.
After Dinner → The Whiskey Bar

2767 Euclid Heights Blvd, Cleveland Heights — 15 min from Soho. Open until 2am Fridays. This is the scotch night — The Whiskey Bar is closed Sundays, so Friday is the right call.

Plan a rideshare (Uber/Lyft) back to the Airbnb from here — don't drive after a scotch flight.

🌙 Jet Lag Tip (SFO → CLE)
Wed–Thu
Try going to bed 1–2 hrs earlier than usual. 3-hour time difference (ET is ahead).
Goal: feel sharp for 8:30am Saturday hike start.
Friday
Early flight recommended — arrive by 4pm gives buffer before dinner at 6:30.
Stay up until 11pm ET to lock in the time zone.
Saturday
Alarm at 7:30am. Brandywine Falls starts at 8:30 — early start beats the crowds.
Brew coffee before heading out, there's no good café near the trailhead.

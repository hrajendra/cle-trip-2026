Fri · 9:00 PM Scotch & Whiskey Bar
The Whiskey Bar
A wall of scotch. A bartender who knows it. That's the whole pitch.
Cleveland Heights institution. Not a cocktail bar — a whisky bar. Come with intent. The bartenders have guided hundreds of flights and genuinely enjoy the process. Let them lead.
🥃 Scotch Strategy
Tell them your entry point — "I like Glenfiddich 12" or "I usually drink Laphroaig" — and they'll build from there
Ask for a guided flight of 3 — they'll pick a progression. Palate cleanser between each.
Regions to explore: Speyside (fruity, floral), Highland (rich, complex), Islay (peaty, smoky), Lowland (light, grassy)
If you want to push it: ask for something you've never heard of — they stock bottles you won't find at a regular bar

Sat · 7:00 PM Gin Bar + Cider House
Griffin Cider House & Gin Bar
The gin night. Come with curiosity.
Lakewood neighbourhood pub that takes gin as seriously as The Whiskey Bar takes scotch. Cozy, dimly lit, bar up front and a lounge in the back. Bartenders are genuinely into it — tell them what you know and they'll take you somewhere interesting.
🍸 Gin Strategy
Styles to know: London Dry (classic, juniper-forward), Contemporary (floral, citrus), Old Tom (slightly sweeter), Navy Strength (high ABV, punchy)
Ask for a gin flight — tell them "we want to understand the range"
Cocktail recs: G&T with a specific tonic pairing, Negroni if they're making one well, Gimlet for something clean
Also order: a cider flight — the cider program is legit and worth trying alongside gin
Sit at the bar first, move to back lounge when you've settled in
